Veri Silme ve Kullanıcı Hakları

  • Uygulama içi silme: Ayarlar → "Tüm Verilerimi Sil" butonuna basarak tüm verileri kalıcı olarak silebilirsiniz. Bu işlem veritabanını, tercihleri, Google hesap bağlantısını ve arka plan işlerini temizler.
  • Uygulamayı kaldırma: Cihazınızdan kaldırdığınızda tüm yerel veriler otomatik olarak silinir.
  • Google Drive yedekleri: Google Drive ayarlarından uygulama verilerini manuel olarak silebilirsiniz.
  • İletişim yoluyla talep: arcelektronik01@gmail.com adresine e-posta göndererek veri silme talebinde bulunabilirsiniz. Talepler 7 iş günü içinde yanıtlanır.

Introduction

ARC Digital Assistant ("App") is a digital ledger application designed for small businesses and tradespeople. This privacy policy describes what data the App collects, how it is stored, how it is protected, and what rights users have.

It has been prepared in accordance with Google Play Developer Program Policies and applicable data-protection regulations.

2

Data Collected and Purposes

💰 Financial Data Required Not Shared Income, expense, debt, receivable records — Core app function
👥 Contacts Optional Not Shared Name, phone number — Easier customer entry. READ_CONTACTS permission can be denied.
📷 Camera Optional Not Shared Receipt/invoice photos — CAMERA permission can be denied.
🔑 Google Account Optional Not Shared Email address — Authentication for Google Drive backup.
⚙️ App Preferences Automatic Not Shared Language, currency, theme, lock setting — Personalizing user experience.
❌ Data we do NOT collect: Location data, advertising identifiers, usage analytics, search/browsing history, audio/video recordings, health/biometric data. The app does not contain any analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or third-party tracking tool.

All financial records are stored only on the user's device. Cloud backup is optional and uploads only to the user's own Google Drive app-specific folder (drive.appdata). Only ARC Digital Assistant can access this folder.

The app database is excluded from Android automatic backup. Only general preferences are included in auto backup.

4

Third-Party Sharing

  • User data is never shared with, sold to, rented to, or transferred to any third party.
  • The app contains no advertisements and sends no data to ad networks.
  • The app contains no analytics or tracking SDK.
  • Google Drive backup writes only to the user's own account; the developer cannot access this data.
5

Data Deletion and User Rights

  • In-app deletion: Go to Settings → "Delete All My Data" to permanently delete all data. This clears the database, preferences, Google account connection, and pending background tasks.
  • Uninstalling the app: Removing the app from your device automatically deletes all local data.
  • Google Drive backups: Users can manually delete app data from Google Drive settings.
  • Request via contact: Send a data deletion request to arcelektronik01@gmail.com. Requests are responded to within 7 business days.
6

Data Retention

Data remains on the device until the user deletes it or uninstalls the app. No user data is stored on developer servers — the app operates entirely offline. Google Drive backups remain in the user's own account and can be deleted by the user.

7

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13 years of age. If we learn that a child has provided data, we will promptly delete it.
